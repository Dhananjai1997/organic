About Us

Established in May 2018, Organic is a remarkable venture stemming from the visionary WHO group, which came into being in 2011. Our primary focus lies in providing exceptional food services that leave an indelible mark. Steeped in the food industry's experience, Pisum Foods has emerged as a leading exporter, specializing in an extensive range of Indian culinary treasures. From aromatic spices to wholesome pulses, grains, fruits, and vegetables, our offerings are a testament to our commitment to quality. What truly distinguishes Pisum Foods is our global reach and enduring relationships. Our products traverse borders, finding homes in various corners of the world. We take immense pride in being a conduit between India's agricultural prowess and international buyers with discerning tastes. Our journey is marked by a steadfast dedication to authenticity and excellence. Each shipment represents not just products, but the passion of our farmers and artisans. Quality, trust, and efficiency are the hallmarks of our operations, ensuring that every product reaching you carries the essence of our values. Organic Foods is more than a business; it's an expedition through flavors and cultures. Our commitment to integrity and innovation continues to guide us. We invite you to embark on this journey with us, where culinary excellence knows no boundaries, and each product resonates with the authenticity and dedication that define us.

Mission and Vision

Mission

our mission is to cultivate a harmonious coexistence between nature and agriculture. We are committed to producing wholesome, high-quality food while respecting the environment and promoting the well-being of all living organisms. Through sustainable and regenerative practices, we aim to nurture the Earth's ecosystems, improve soil health, and safeguard the health of our consumers.

Vision

our vision is to lead the way in creating a sustainable and thriving global food ecosystem through the principles of organic agriculture. We envision a future where organic farming is not just a choice, but a fundamental cornerstone of agricultural systems worldwide.

Organic.

Organic farming is the production of food without the use of synthetic chemicals or genetically modified components.
